  • Your are completely free about the content of your page (you can make one single long page with content, multiple pages)
  • in the end, you should upload your page to github and try to present your own page in front of the class (on next wednesday)

hints before starting

  • Start from a user's perspective -- (I is in the following case your user):
    • Who am I?
    • What do I want? / What am I looking for?
    • How do I get to your website?
    • What should I do in the end when vistting your website? / What should I feel?
  • Put it all together -- What is the STORY of the visitor until nhe reaches you out
  • suggestion: start with the "mobile first" approach -- the mobile view of your page is the simple one, because it is only one single column, the design for the desktop is the hard one
